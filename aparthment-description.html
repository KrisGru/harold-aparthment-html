About this apartment

The Royal Apartment is a beautifully designed space located in the heart of Kraków's Old Town, just steps away from the Main Market Square. This elegant apartment combines historic charm with modern comforts.

With two comfortable bedrooms and a spacious living area, it's perfect for couples, families or small groups looking to experience the best of Kraków in style. The apartment features high ceilings, original architectural details, and carefully selected furnishings that create a warm and inviting atmosphere.

The fully equipped kitchen allows you to prepare meals at home, while the comfortable living area is perfect for relaxing after a day of exploring the city. The bedrooms feature premium bedding to ensure a good night's sleep.

Location

The apartment is located in the very center of Kraków's Old Town, just a 2-minute walk from the Main Market Square. You'll be surrounded by historic landmarks, excellent restaurants, and vibrant nightlife.

Nearby attractions include Wawel Castle (10 min walk), the Jewish Quarter (15 min walk), and the Vistula River boulevards (5 min walk). Public transportation options are abundant, with tram and bus stops within a 5-minute walk.
